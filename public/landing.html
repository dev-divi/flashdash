Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. 14 days, full access, no credit card required. If it doesn't save you time and money in two weeks, don't pay.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. FlashDash stores everything locally on your device using IndexedDB. Create jobs, generate invoices, track status — all offline. Data syncs when you're back online.

Can I use my own Stripe link for payments?

Yes. Add your Stripe payment link in Settings. It gets embedded in every invoice PDF so clients can pay with one click.

What kind of businesses use FlashDash?

Auto detailers, mobile mechanics, cleaning services, handymen, landscapers, photographers — anyone who does service work in the field and needs to track jobs and send invoices fast.

What's the difference between FlashDash and SoloCraft?

SoloCraft is a free invoice and proposal generator — great if you just need to create PDFs. FlashDash goes further: job creation, status tracking, service presets, offline mode, and one-tap invoicing. If you're running a service business with multiple jobs per day, FlashDash is built for you.
